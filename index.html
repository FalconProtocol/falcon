HOW DOES IT WORK?

  1. Alice from USA wants to send €100 to Gertrude in Germany
  2. Alice's bank asks Gertrude's bank how much Bitcoin it needs to send them so they would send €100 to Gertrude
  3. Gertrude's bank gets a quote from a liquidity provider (฿0.23), and passes it, along with a bitcoin address, back to Alice's bank.
  4. Alice's bank gets a quote from its liquidity provider for buying ฿0.23 ($130)
  5. Alice confirms the transaction, and Alice's bank uses Alice's US$ in her account to exercise the quote.
  6. It sends ฿0.23 to the bitcoin address provided by Gertrude's bank.
  7. Gertrude's bank receives the bitcoin and executes the quote
  8. Gertrude gets her €100

All faster than you can say "the airspeed velocity of an unladen swift"

About

International money transfer has been slow, expensive and complicated. Until now.

Falcon is the best way to send money from one point to another.
It's built for speed using standard web technologies and is available for anyone to use.
It is designed to run on bitcoin, but it can be adapted for other crypto-currencies too.

Fast. Secure. Ultra-affordable. And open. Falcon is the future of money.
